Tasty 10 minute mac and cheese that's sure to delight your taste buds

You get home from a long day of work, you're exhausted and have no energy for anything else, I'm presenting to you how to make mac and cheese

Ingredients:

- 1 box of kraft mac and cheese

3 cups of water

Directions:

Bring water to a boil, add salt. Once the water is boiling, add the pasta and bring down to medium heat. Cook for 7-8 minutes or until tender

Carefully drain the pasta, then reintroduce to the pot. Bring the stove top to low and add 1 cup of milk and add cheese mix, stir.
